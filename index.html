About

Hi, I'm John Michael (yes, I have two first names!). I am an aspiring software engineer interested in web development and distributed computing. I am starting at Amazon in March 2019 as a software development engineer and I could not be more excited. I recently graduated from the University of Georgia with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

I am a passionate coder and technologist that is always hungry to learn more. I am a fairly heavy Java user, but am also proficient with Python, Javascript, and C++, and enjoy frameworks such as Spring Boot, Django, Express for server-side development. I am playing around with React a bit in order to be able to have a wholistic understanding of the modern web stack from top-to-bottom. I am currently focusing on learning more about operating systems and distributed systems, and hope to complete serveral Coursera courses on these subjects in the coming months.

Check out my github for a detailed look at some of my projects. I've built projects ranging across a variety of fields in computer science, including web development, natural language processing, networking, and operating systems. For example, I performed NLP research on the effects of financial news on the stock market, built a fully functioning e-commerce website, and implemented over a dozen of the most useful UNIX utilities from scratch. These are just some examples of the stuff I'm interested in, and I'm always trying to contribute more. My next goal is to complete a React Native project I've been excited about for quite a while.

Outside of software, I enjoy soccer (go Gunners!), traveling (I expect to travel to Asia in early 2019), and listening to music. I am currently trying to learn piano, but have had to take a break because of an intense school semester. Thankfully, I will be able to focus on piano consistently now that I am almost graduated (hooray!).
